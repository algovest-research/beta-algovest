Methodology
  1. Lump sum + SIP: Lump sum compounded annually. SIP contributions compounded from each year they were added using XIRR-style accumulation.
  2. Tax: Applied to gains only — actual income slab for FD/LIC (0–30%), 12.5% LTCG for equity (Budget 2024 rates).
  3. City-adjusted inflation: National CPI from World Bank × city premium from RBI regional CPI data.
  4. INR depreciation: Annual average USD/INR rates from RBI DBIE, applied year-by-year.
  5. S&P 500: Historical S&P 500 annualised USD return from start year, converted to INR at today's rate (₹96.5/$1).
  6. Gold: MCX historical CAGR (~10–14%). Real estate: NHB Residex metro avg (~7–10%).
  7. Retirement projection: Current real corpus projected to age 60 at current real CAGR vs AlgoVest est. 11% real CAGR.

Data: World Bank CPI India · RBI DBIE INR/USD · Nifty 50 historical data · MCX gold · NHB Residex. Figures are estimates for illustration only.
